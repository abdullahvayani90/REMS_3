System Summary

Welcome to your Real Estate Management System Version 2.0. This system operates on a commercial Agency/Brokerage Model. Revenue is calculated strictly based on the Agency Commission earned from closed deals, rather than the total market value of the properties traded. Client data is automatically managed and de-duplicated based on unique phone numbers to ensure a clean directory.
